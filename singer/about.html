MLM singer - Mamoun Laraqui

Franch-Moroccan performer with inimitable voice, granular and powerful


Biography

After a childhood in a French high school in the Moroccan capital and obtaining a bachelor's degree in science, Mamoun decided to reorient himself towards higher studies in applied arts. Master in visual communication in his pocket, he emigrates a few months in China to discover a new culture and learn the language, it is there that he decides to create his own brand of fashion accessories (www.fkbrands. com). During all these years, Mamoun has always been passionate about singing without really being aware of its potential. This awareness will take place the day he decides to start singing lessons at the beginning of 2013. Since that day, singing and stage have become his top priority.
